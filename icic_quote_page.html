Smart Care garages in my city

Your car is often your second home. With Smart Saver, we'll be there to help you find a local Smart Care garage you can trust, in no time.

Why go to a Smart Saver garage?

When you raise a claim with us be assured, we'll guide you through each step of the process and expedite your claim with our AI-assisted system.

  • No waiting around

    The minute you inform us that you’re in trouble, a Smart Saver executive will pick up your car from the accident spot and take it to the nearest Smart Care garage.

  • ₹5,000 deductible waived off

    You don’t have to pay ₹5,000 at the time of claim. Thus, you end up saving not only when you buy this plan but also when you claim.

  • Guarantee on repairs

    All servicing & repairs come with a warranty for 6 months or 6000 km, whichever is earlier – for complete peace of mind.

  • Travel allowance for delays

    Your car is repaired in 3 days or less, otherwise receive a travel allowance of ₹500 per day for up to 5 days.

To understand this further, let’s look at a hypothetical claim scenario*.

Nikhil’s car is damaged in an accident and is not in a drivable condition. He doesn’t inform on 1800 2666 about it and gets it towed to a non-Smart Saver garage. There, the repairs take over 3 days and he ends up taking cab rides as a transport alternative. Here’s what he incurs as out-of-pocket expenses vs had he chosen to go to a Smart Saver garage.

Non-Smart Saver garage Smart Saver garage
Towing fees ₹1,000 ₹0
Cab fare when your car takes more than 3 days for repairs ₹500/day ₹0 up to 5 days
Repair cost ₹8,000 ₹8,000

*Assuming Nikhil has Zero Depreciation cover and Consumables cover in his policy along with Smart Saver. He would also bear a mandatory deductible of ₹1,000.

Important rules for your claim

Keep these in mind when you need to avail the Smart Saver garages benefits a waiver on deductible.

  • Inform us on 1800 2666 as soon as you need to claim
  • Ensure your damaged car is taken to a Smart Saver garage
